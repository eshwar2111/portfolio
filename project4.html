Cross-Modal Synthesis GAN

Developed a GAN-based model for cross-modal synthesis, converting MRI images to CT scans and vice versa. The model leverages Pix2Pix architecture to facilitate data augmentation in medical imaging, improving diagnostic capabilities in healthcare.

Technologies Used:

  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
  • Pix2Pix Architecture
  • PyTorch
  • Medical Imaging (MRI, CT)
