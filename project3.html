TripSync - AI Travel Assistant

TripSync is an AI-based travel assistant that helps tourists plan customized itineraries based on their preferences, interests, and travel history. The app generates an optimized schedule for exploring cities and tourist attractions based on user input.

Technologies Used:

  • Machine Learning Algorithms
